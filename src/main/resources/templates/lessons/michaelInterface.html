The purchase price of a taxable item is computed from its list price and its tax rate. The purchase price of a nontaxable item is simply its list price.     For example, if a vehicle has a dealer cost of $20,000.00, a deal markup of $2,500.00, and a tax rate of 0.10, then the list price of the vehicle would be $22,500.00 and the purchase price (including tax) would be $24,750.00.
